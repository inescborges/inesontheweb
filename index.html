I'm working in the tech field for over two years as a frontend developer. The main reason why I started this was due to my old interest in design, motion graphics and animations.

Since now everyone acquires information about whatever they want to know mostly on the web, I think it would be nice for this virtual world to get more inspiring and cleaner than it is. Sometimes it's hard to put my ideas in a visible way, but I keep on trying. Discussing them with people is always the best help I can get.

But it's not just the visuals that interest me. I'm also a supporter of valuable content. I'm here to help make the world better. I love to share new ideas and to know other people's. I love my music and headphones. I love to write as a therapy. Welcome to my little space.
